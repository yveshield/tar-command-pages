Prayer for Victory over Enemies.

For the music director. A Psalm of David.

1 May the LORD answer you on a day of trouble!
May the name of the God of Jacob aprotect you!

2May He send you help from the sanctuary,
And support you from Zion!

3May He remember all your meal offerings
And baccept your burnt offering! Selah

4 May He grant you your heart’s desire
And fulfill your whole plan!

5cWe will sing for joy over your dvictory,
And in the name of our God we will set up our banners.
May the LORD fulfill all your desires.

6 Now I know that the LORD saves His anointed;
He will answer him from His holy heaven
With the esaving strength of His right hand.

7Some praise their chariots and some their horses,

8They have bowed down and fallen,
But we have risen and stood upright.

9fSave, LORD;
May the King answer us on the day we call.

Daniel Is Terrified by a Vision

Daniel Comforted

10 Then behold, a hand touched me and shook me on my hhands and knees. 11And he said to me, “Daniel, iyou who are treasured, understand the words that I am about to tell you and stand at your place, for I have now been sent to you.” And when he had spoken this word to me, I stood up trembling. 12Then he said to me, “Do not be afraid, Daniel, for from the first day that you set your heart on understanding this and on humbling yourself before your God, your words were heard, and I have come in response to your words. 13But the prince of the kingdom of Persia was standing jin my way for twenty-one days; then behold, Michael, one of the chief princes, came to help me, for I had been left there with the kings of Persia. 14Now I have come to explain to you what will happen to your people in the klatter days, because the vision pertains to the days still future.”

15 When he had spoken to me according to these words, I lturned my face toward the ground and became speechless. 16And behold, mone who resembled a human was touching my lips.

Psalm 75 [Books]

God Humbles the Proud, but Exalts the Righteous.

For the music director; set to aAl-tashheth. A Psalm of Asaph, a Song.

1 We give thanks to You, God, we give thanks,
For Your name is near;
People declare Your wondrous works.

2“When I select an appointed time,
It is I who judge fairly.

3The earth and all who inhabit it bare unsteady;
It is I who have firmly set its pillars. Selah

4I said to the boastful, ‘Do not boast,’
And to the wicked, ‘Do not lift up the horn;

5Do not lift up your horn on high,
Do not speak with insolent cpride.’ ”

6 For not from the east, nor from the west,
Nor from the ddesert comes exaltation;

7But God is the Judge;
He puts down one and exalts another.

8For a cup is in the hand of the LORD, and the wine foams;
It is ewell mixed, and He pours out of this;
Certainly all the wicked of the earth must drain and drink its dregs.

9 But as for me, I will declare it forever;
I will sing praises to the God of Jacob.

10And fHe will cut off all the horns of the wicked,
But the horns of the righteous will be lifted up.

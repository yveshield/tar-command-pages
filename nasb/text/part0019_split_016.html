Psalm 100 [Books]

All People Exhorted to Praise God.

A Psalm for aThanksgiving.

1 Shout joyfully to the LORD, all the earth.

2Serve the LORD with jubilation;
Come before Him with rejoicing.

3Know that the LORD bHimself is God;
It is He who has made us, and cnot we ourselves;
We are His people and the sheep of His pasture.

4 Enter His gates with dthanksgiving,
And His courtyards with praise.
Give thanks to Him, bless His name.

5For the LORD is good;
His mercy is everlasting
And His faithfulness is to all generations.

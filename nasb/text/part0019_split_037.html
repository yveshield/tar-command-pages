Psalm 121 [Books]

The LORD, the Keeper of Israel.

A Song of Ascents.

1 I will raise my eyes to the mountains;
From where will my help come?

2My help comes from the LORD,
Who made heaven and earth.

3He will not allow your foot to slip;
He who watches over you will not slumber.

4Behold, He who watches over Israel
Will neither slumber nor sleep.

5 The LORD is your aprotector;
The LORD is your shade on your right hand.

6The sun will not bbeat down on you by day,
Nor the moon by night.

7The LORD will cprotect you from all evil;
He will keep your soul.

8The LORD will dguard your going out and your coming in
From this time and forever.

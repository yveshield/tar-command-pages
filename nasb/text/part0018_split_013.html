A Contrite Sinner’s Prayer for Pardon.

For the music director. A Psalm of David, when aNathan the prophet came to him, after he had gone in to Bathsheba.

1 Be gracious to me, God, according to Your faithfulness;
According to the greatness of Your compassion, wipe out my wrongdoings.

2Wash me thoroughly from my guilt
And cleanse me from my sin.

3For I know my wrongdoings,
And my sin is constantly before me.

4Against You, You only, I have sinned
And done what is evil in Your sight,
So that You bare justified cwhen You speak
And dblameless when You judge.

5 Behold, I was brought forth in guilt,
And in sin my mother conceived me.

6Behold, You desire truth in the einnermost being,
And in secret You will make wisdom known to me.

7fPurify me with hyssop, and I will be clean;
gCleanse me, and I will be whiter than snow.

8hLet me hear joy and gladness,
Let the bones You have broken rejoice.

9Hide Your face from my sins
And wipe out all my guilty deeds.

10 Create iin me a clean heart, God,
And renew a steadfast spirit within me.

11Do not cast me away from Your presence,
And do not take Your Holy Spirit from me.

12Restore to me the joy of Your salvation,
And sustain me with a willing spirit.

13Then I will teach wrongdoers Your ways,
And sinners will jbe converted to You.

14 Save me from the guilt of bloodshed, God, the God of my salvation;
Then my tongue will joyfully sing of Your righteousness.

15Lord, kopen my lips,
So that my mouth may declare Your praise.

16For You do not delight in sacrifice, otherwise I would give it;
You do not take pleasure in burnt offering.

17The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit;
A broken and a contrite heart, God, You will not despise.

18 By Your favor do good to Zion;
lBuild the walls of Jerusalem.

19Then You will delight in mrighteous sacrifices,
In burnt offering and whole burnt offering;
Then nbulls will be offered on Your altar.

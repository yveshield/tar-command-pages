Psalm 61 [Books]

Confidence in God’s Protection.

For the music director; on a stringed instrument. A Psalm of David.

1 Hear my cry, God;
Give Your attention to my prayer.

2From the end of the earth I call to You when my heart is faint;
Lead me to the rock that is higher than I.

3For You have been a refuge for me,
A tower of strength against the enemy.

4Let me adwell in Your tent forever;
Let me take refuge in the shelter of Your wings. Selah

5 For You have heard my vows, God;
You have given me the inheritance of those who fear Your name.

6You will bprolong the king’s clife;
His years will be like generations.

7He will dsit enthroned before God forever;
Appoint faithfulness and truth that they may watch over him.

8So I will sing praise to Your name forever,
That I may pay my vows day by day.

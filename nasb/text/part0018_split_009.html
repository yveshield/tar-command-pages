Psalm 47 [Books]

God, the King of the Earth.

For the music director. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.

1 Clap your hands, all you peoples;
Shout to God with a voice of joy.

2For the LORD Most High is to be feared,
A great King over all the earth.

3He subdues peoples under us
And nations under our feet.

4He chooses our inheritance for us,
The pride of Jacob whom He loves. Selah

5 God has ascended awith a shout,
The LORD, awith the sound of a trumpet.

6Sing praises to God, sing praises;
Sing praises to our King, sing praises.

7For God is the King of all the earth;
Sing praises with a psalm of wisdom.

8God reigns over the nations,
God bsits on His holy throne.

9The cprinces of the people have assembled as the people of the God of Abraham,
For the shields of the earth belong to God;
He is highly exalted.
